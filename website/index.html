§1.0

Abstract

Mission brief

Parvaz is an Android VPN app. It routes every byte of phone traffic through a Google Apps Script relay you deploy yourself, using the domain-fronting protocol rewritten from MasterHttpRelayVPN — TLS SNI www.google.com, HTTP Host script.google.com. DPI only sees "traffic to google.com"; Google routes internally to your Apps Script deployment.

One APK. One Connect button. Your relay. No Python runtime, no shared server, no telemetry, no Play Store. Kotlin + Compose front end. Go SOCKS5 core. Both open source, MIT-licensed.

Notice to airmen

Restricted airspace
Caution · Restricted airspace NOTAM A0001/26
Google Apps Script's terms forbid this use. Deploy the relay script to your own Google account only — do not centralize deployments or distribute a shared one. Personal, research, and educational use only. If you do not understand the legal implications, do not deploy.

Airframe specifications

Features
01Domain frontingTLS SNI says www.google.com. HTTP Host says script.google.com. DPI sees the front; Google routes by the real Host internally.
02One-tap VPNPaste your relay URL + access key, tap Connect. VpnService captures all phone traffic — no per-app configuration, no browser proxy setup.
03Open source APKKotlin + Compose front end, embedded Go SOCKS5 core. No proprietary dependencies. Ships outside the Play Store — F-Droid and sideload only.
04Light-theme field manualNOTAM aesthetic. Redaction, JetBrains Mono, and Vazirmatn typography. Persian UI built in — first-class RTL, not an afterthought.
05h2 + batchingHTTP/2 multiplexed transport with connection pooling and request batching, mirroring upstream's performance characteristics.
06Zero telemetryNo analytics, no auto-update pings, no crash reporting. Your access_key stays in EncryptedSharedPreferences. No third-party network calls other than the relay itself.

§7.0

Ground support

Required · upstream

Parvaz replaces only the client half of MasterHttpRelayVPN. The Apps Script server stays exactly as upstream ships it. See reference/apps_script/Code.gs and upstream deployment steps to deploy the relay on your own Google account. Without a deployment, Parvaz has nothing to tunnel to.
